Silvarium Social — Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Silvarium Social is a social media management and analytics tool that allows users to connect multiple third-party platforms in one dashboard to manage and analyze their accounts.

Information We Collect

  • Account and basic profile data provided by the user
  • Authorized data from connected third-party platforms via official APIs
  • Usage and technical information for security and performance

How We Use Information

Data is used solely for management, analytics, reporting, security, and service improvement.

Third-Party Platforms

Silvarium Social accesses third-party platform data only with explicit user authorization and strictly within the permissions granted. We do not sell data, bypass safeguards, or access private content beyond approved scopes.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their data at: https://www.silvariumsocial.com/data-deletion

Contact

Email: support@silvariumsocial.com
